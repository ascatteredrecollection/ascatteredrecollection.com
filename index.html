Holding Space

This space is a continuous conversation about anything—a place to hold thoughts that usually turn to dust.

The "ghost" is not about anonymity, but about universality. These scattered recollections belong to anyone who has ever felt caught between worlds, who carries memories like precious cargo, and who believes that even the smallest trace can echo through time.

"Time is a present. Don't forget that in the present, you have the time."
